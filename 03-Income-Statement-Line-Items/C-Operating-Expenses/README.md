## Overview: Operating Expenses

### Definition

Costs required to run the business that cannot be directly attributed to specific jobs.

Also called:
- General and Administrative (G&A) Expenses
- Overhead
- Indirect Costs
- SG&A (Selling, General & Administrative)

---

### G&A vs. Direct Costs

**The Test:** Can this cost be directly traced to a specific job?
- YES → Direct Cost (Cost of Revenue)
- NO → G&A (Operating Expense)

---

### G&A Expense Categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Personnel** | Officers, office staff, HR |
| **Facility** | Rent, utilities, maintenance |
| **Insurance** | General liability, auto, umbrella |
| **Professional** | Accounting, legal, consulting |
| **Office** | Supplies, postage, telephone |
| **Depreciation** | Office equipment, vehicles |
| **Other** | Dues, training, bad debts |

---

### G&A as Percentage of Revenue

**Industry Benchmarks:**

| Company Size | Typical G&A % |
|--------------|---------------|
| Under $5M revenue | 10-15% |
| $5M - $25M | 8-12% |
| $25M - $100M | 6-10% |
| Over $100M | 4-8% |

**Higher G&A may indicate:**
- Inefficiency
- Heavy investment in growth
- Complex operations
- Start-up phase

## 1. Personnel Costs

### Officers' Salaries

**Definition:** Compensation paid to corporate officers (President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer).

**Components:**
- Base salary
- Bonuses
- Employer payroll taxes
- Benefits

**Special Considerations:**
- S Corp reasonable compensation requirement
- Surety scrutiny of officer compensation
- Related party considerations

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Officers' Salaries                  200,000
Dr. Payroll Taxes - Officers             15,300
    Cr. Cash                                   180,000
    Cr. Payroll Tax Liabilities                 35,300
```

---

### Office Salaries and Wages

**Definition:** Compensation for administrative and support staff.

**Common Positions:**
- Office manager
- Bookkeeper/accountant
- Administrative assistant
- Receptionist
- HR personnel
- Estimators (if not job-specific)
- Project managers (if not job-specific)

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Office Salaries                     150,000
    Cr. Cash                                   125,000
    Cr. Payroll Tax Liabilities                 25,000
```

---

### Payroll Taxes and Benefits

**Employer Payroll Taxes:**
| Tax | Rate | Cap |
|-----|------|-----|
| Social Security | 6.2% | $160,200 (2023) |
| Medicare | 1.45% | None |
| FUTA | 0.6% | $7,000 |
| SUTA | Varies | Varies by state |

**Benefits:**
- Health insurance
- Dental/vision
- 401(k) match
- Life insurance
- Disability insurance
- Paid time off

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Payroll Tax Expense                  35,000
Dr. Employee Benefits                    75,000
    Cr. Payroll Tax Liabilities                 35,000
    Cr. Benefits Payable                        75,000
```

---

### Income Statement Presentation

```
GENERAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES:
  Officers' Salaries                      $215,300
  Office Salaries and Wages                175,000
  Payroll Taxes and Benefits               110,000
```

## 3. Insurance

### General Liability Insurance

**Definition:** Coverage for bodily injury, property damage, personal injury claims.

**Typical Coverage:**
- Per occurrence limit
- General aggregate
- Products/completed operations

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Insurance Expense - G&A              45,000
    Cr. Prepaid Insurance                       45,000
```

Or if paid in installments:
```
Dr. Insurance Expense - G&A               3,750
    Cr. Cash                                     3,750
```

---

### Auto Insurance

**Definition:** Coverage for company vehicles.

**Types:**
- Liability
- Collision
- Comprehensive
- Uninsured motorist

---

### Workers' Compensation Insurance

**Definition:** Coverage for employee injuries.

**Note:** May be allocated to jobs based on labor, or kept in G&A.

**Initial Premium:**
```
Dr. Prepaid Insurance                   100,000
    Cr. Cash                                   100,000
```

**Monthly Amortization:**
```
Dr. Insurance Expense - G&A               8,333
    Cr. Prepaid Insurance                        8,333
```

---

### Umbrella/Excess Liability

**Definition:** Additional liability coverage above primary policies.

---

### Income Statement Presentation

```
GENERAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES:
  Insurance                               $175,000
```

Or detailed:
```
GENERAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES - INSURANCE:
  General Liability                    $45,000
  Workers' Compensation                 85,000
  Auto Insurance                        25,000
  Umbrella/Excess                       15,000
  Other Insurance                        5,000
                                      ─────────
  Total Insurance                     $175,000
```

## 6. Depreciation and Amortization

### Depreciation Expense

**Definition:** Allocation of fixed asset cost over useful life.

**G&A Depreciation Includes:**
- Office building
- Office furniture
- Office equipment
- Non-job-specific vehicles
- Leasehold improvements

**Note:** Job-specific equipment depreciation may be Direct Cost.

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Depreciation Expense - G&A           45,000
    Cr. Accumulated Depreciation                45,000
```

---

### Amortization Expense

**Definition:** Allocation of intangible asset cost over useful life.

**Examples:**
- Goodwill (if impaired)
- Non-compete agreements
- Software development costs
- Organizational costs

**Journal Entry:**
```
Dr. Amortization Expense                  5,000
    Cr. Accumulated Amortization                 5,000
```

---

### Income Statement Presentation

```
GENERAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES:
  Depreciation                            $45,000
  Amortization                              5,000
```

## 7. Other G&A Expenses

**Definition:** Estimated uncollectible receivables.

**Methods:**
- Percentage of receivables
- Aging method
- Specific identification

**Journal Entry:**
```

    Cr. Allowance for Doubtful Accounts         15,000
```

---

**Definition:** Membership fees and publications.

## Summary: G&A Key Points

| Category | Key Considerations |
|----------|-------------------|
| Personnel | Largest G&A category typically |
| Facility | Match to leases, verify allocation |
| Insurance | Separate job vs. G&A insurance |
| Professional | Accrue for known services |
| Office | Small but numerous accounts |
| Depreciation | Proper asset classification |
| Other | Monitor "miscellaneous" |
