# Employee Benefits Disclosures

## Overview

Construction companies provide various employee benefits, including retirement plans, health insurance, and other benefits. These disclosures cover defined contribution plans, defined benefit pension plans, multiemployer plans, and other post-employment benefits.

### Template 2: Multiemployer Plan - Detailed

**Multiemployer Pension Plans**

The Company contributes to multiemployer defined benefit pension plans under collective bargaining agreements. The risks of participating in multiemployer plans differ from single-employer plans:

- Assets contributed to a multiemployer plan may be used to provide benefits to employees of other participating employers
- If a participating employer withdraws from the plan, the unfunded obligations may be borne by remaining participating employers
- If the Company withdraws from the plan, it may be required to pay a withdrawal liability
