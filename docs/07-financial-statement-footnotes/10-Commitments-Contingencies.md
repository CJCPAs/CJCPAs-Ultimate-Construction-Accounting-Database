# Commitments and Contingencies Disclosures

## Overview

Construction companies face numerous commitments and contingencies, including litigation, claims, warranties, purchase commitments, and various contractual obligations. ASC 450 (Contingencies) and ASC 440 (Commitments) provide guidance on recognition and disclosure.

---

## General Contingencies Disclosure

### Template: Standard Contingencies Policy

**Contingencies**

The Company accrues for loss contingencies when it is probable that a liability has been incurred and the amount of loss can be reasonably estimated. When a range of loss is probable and no amount within the range is a better estimate than any other, the Company accrues the minimum amount in the range. Legal fees and other costs related to contingencies are expensed as incurred.

The Company discloses contingencies when there is a reasonable possibility that a loss may have been incurred, even if the loss is not probable or cannot be reasonably estimated.
