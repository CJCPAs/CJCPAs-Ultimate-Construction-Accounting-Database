# Related Party Transactions Disclosures

## Overview

Related party disclosures are particularly important for construction companies, which often involve ownership by multiple family members, transactions with affiliated entities, and various arrangements between owners and the company. ASC 850 requires disclosure of material related party transactions.

---

## General Related Party Policy

### Template: Related Party Policy Disclosure

**Related Party Transactions**

The Company enters into various transactions with related parties in the ordinary course of business. Related parties include:

- Officers and directors of the Company
- Principal [shareholders | members | partners] and their immediate families
- Entities owned or controlled by officers, directors, or principal owners
- Joint ventures and other entities in which the Company has significant influence
- Key management personnel

Related party transactions are conducted on terms that management believes are comparable to those that would be available from unrelated parties. The Company's Board of Directors [or governing body] reviews and approves all material related party transactions.
