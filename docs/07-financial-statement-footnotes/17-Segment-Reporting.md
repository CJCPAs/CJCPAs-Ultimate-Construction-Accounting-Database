# Segment Reporting Disclosures

## Overview

ASC 280, Segment Reporting, requires disclosure of information about operating segments for public companies. While many construction companies are privately held and not subject to these requirements, some may voluntarily provide segment information or be required to under certain circumstances.

---

### Template: Single Segment Conclusion

**Segment Information**

Management has evaluated the Company's operations and determined that the Company operates as a single reportable segment. This conclusion is based on the following factors:

- The chief operating decision maker (CODM) reviews consolidated financial information to assess performance and make resource allocation decisions
- The Company provides similar services to similar customers
- Resources are allocated and performance is evaluated on a company-wide basis
