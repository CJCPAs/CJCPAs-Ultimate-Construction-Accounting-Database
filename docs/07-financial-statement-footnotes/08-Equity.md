# Equity Disclosures

## Overview

Equity disclosures vary significantly based on the entity type (C Corporation, S Corporation, LLC, Partnership). This section provides comprehensive templates for all common entity structures in the construction industry.

## Restrictions on Distributions

### Template: Distribution Restrictions

**Restrictions on Distributions**

The Company's ability to make distributions to [shareholders | members | partners] is subject to the following restrictions:

1. **Credit Agreement Restrictions**: The Company's credit agreement restricts dividends and distributions to an annual amount not exceeding [XX]% of net income, provided no default exists.

2. **Statutory Restrictions**: Under [STATE] law, distributions may not be made if, after giving effect to the distribution:
   - The Company would be unable to pay its debts as they become due in the ordinary course of business; or
   - The Company's total assets would be less than its total liabilities

3. **Bonding Requirements**: The Company's surety requires maintenance of minimum working capital and net worth levels that may effectively limit distributions.
